AtCoder-4900 Engines
2019-11-24|ACM
字数总计: 475|阅读时长: 2 分钟

Problem Statement

E869120 is initially standing at the origin (0,0)(0,0)(0,0) in a two-dimensional plane.

He has N engines, which can be used as follows:

  • When E869120 uses the i-th engine, his X- and Y-coordinate change by xix_ixi​ and yiy_iyi​, respectively. In other words, if E869120 uses the i-th engine from coordinates (X,Y)(X,Y)(X,Y), he will move to the coordinates (X+xi,Y+yi)(X+x_i,Y+y_i)(X+xi​,Y+yi​).
  • E869120 can use these engines in any order, but each engine can be used at most once. He may also choose not to use some of the engines.

He wants to go as far as possible from the origin. Let (X,Y)(X,Y)(X,Y) be his final coordinates. Find the maximum possible value of X2+Y2\sqrt{X^2+Y^2}X2+Y2​ , the distance from the origin.

大致题意

有N个引擎，每个引擎能把当前位置(X,Y)(X,Y)(X,Y)变为(X+xi,Y+yi)(X+x_i,Y+y_i)(X+xi​,Y+yi​)，每个引擎可以选择用或不用，问最远能到多远，即求max⁡X2+Y2\max{\sqrt{X^2+Y^2}}maxX2+Y2​。

思路

极角排序，把每个引擎都想成一个向量，则两个角度相差小的向量相加一定大，对所有向量按照角度排序，遍历所有向量，找出以第iii个向量开始的最远距离。
