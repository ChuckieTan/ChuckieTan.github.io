AtCoder-5037 Consecutive Integers
2019-11-24|ACM
字数总计: 110|阅读时长: 1 分钟

Problem Statement

Snuke has N integers: 1,2,⋯ ,N1,2,\cdots,N1,2,⋯,N. He will choose K of them and give those to Takahashi. How many ways are there to choose K consecutive integers?

大致题意

从NNN个数里面选KKK个连续的数，求有多少种选法。

思路

ans=N−K+1ans = N - K + 1ans=N−K+1
