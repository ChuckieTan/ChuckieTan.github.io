AtCoder-5633 Counting of Trees
2019-11-24|ACM
字数总计: 400|阅读时长: 2 分钟

Problem Statement

Given is an integer sequence D1,⋯ ,DND_1,\cdots,D_ND1​,⋯,DN​ of NNN elements. Find the number, modulo 998244353, of trees with NNN vertices numbered 1 to NNN that satisfy the following condition:

  • For every integer iii from 111 to NNN, the distance between Vertex 111 and Vertex iii is DiD_iDi​.

Notes

  • A tree of NNN vertices is a connected undirected graph with NNN vertices and N−1N−1N−1 edges, and the distance between two vertices are the number of edges in the shortest path between them.
  • Two trees are considered different if and only if there are two vertices xxx and yyy such that there is an edge between xxx and yyy in one of those trees and not in the other.

大致题意
